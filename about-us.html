About Seek Wild Africa

Your trusted safari company in Rwanda and Uganda, bringing you closer to Africa's most breathtaking wildlife and landscapes.

Our Story

From unforgettable Primate trekking adventures in the wild, including Gorilla Trekking, Chimpanzee Trekking, and Golden Monkey Tracking, to classic Big Five safaris in Akagera National Park, Queen Elizabeth National Park, and Murchison Falls National Park, we design personalized journeys that immerse you in the heart of the wild.

We offer exceptional value for budget, mid-range, and luxury safari travelers, with flexible 1-day to multi-day tour options crafted to match every style of adventure.

"We care for wildlife and support local communities, making each journey meaningful and responsible."
